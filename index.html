Homemade Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 medium onion, chopped, about 1 cup
  • 1 large clove garlic, finely chopped
  • Two 24-oz. jars Pasta Sauce
  • One 15-ounce container ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup frozen, chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 15 fully cooked lasagna noodles (approx. 3/4 of a 16 ounce package)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Equipments

  • Skillet
  • Baking pan
  • Pot

Directions

  1. Cook ground beef and pork in a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Stir to break up meat and cook until browned. Remove from skillet and drain off excess fat.
  2. Add onion and garlic to skillet and fry until golden. Stir in pasta sauce along with browned meat mixture; bring to a boil and cook for 2 â€“ 3 minutes more. Remove from heat; set aside to cool for a few minutes.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese and spinach.
  4. Spray oil onto the baking dish. Lay 5 cooked lasagna noodles at the bottom of a greased baking dish. Spoon about one third of the meat mixture on top of lasagna noodles spreading evenly. Top with about half of the ricotta mixture.
  5. Repeat the steps above twice, ending up with meat sauce on top. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees, covered for about 30 to 40 minutes or until heated through. Bake uncovered for 10 minutes more. Sprinkle with freshly chopped parsley if desired.
